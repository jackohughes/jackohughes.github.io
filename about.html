About

I'm a software developer at Virtusales Publishing Solutions. I'm currently working on the Biblio3 suite of technology used by publishing houses such as Penguin Random House, Hachette, and OUP.

My interests lie mainly with programming languages and their implementation - especially compiler optimisation. I am also fascinated by program synthesis! (I wrote an inductive program synthesis engine for my undergraduate project)

I currently live and work in Brighton, in the south of England.

Outside of programming, I enjoy producing ambient music (particuarly with tape loops!). Let me know if you're interested in algorithmically generated music - I have several ideas for cool things to do ;)
